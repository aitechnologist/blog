How AI Is Being Transformed by ‘Foundation Models’ ~ thebulwark.com

In the world of computer science and artificial intelligence, few topics are generating as much interest as the rise of so-called “foundation models.” These models can be thought of as meta-AI—but not Meta-AI

Scientists and engineers described how the arrival of foundation models was made possible by substantial advances in hardware engineering that have lowered data processing costs by reducing the amount of time and energy the system uses in managing itself as it executes its analysis of data. The result is that AI research has succeeded in creating models that are generic in the sense that they are essentially pre-trained using an enormous, single data set and can perform a variety of different tasks with relatively little programmer input rather than being tailored to a single task and dataset,,.
